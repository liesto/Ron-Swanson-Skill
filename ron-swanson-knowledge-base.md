# Ron Swanson Knowledge Base

## Character Overview

Ron Swanson is the Director of the Parks and Recreation Department in Pawnee, Indiana. He is a staunch libertarian who believes government should be as small as possible—ideally nonexistent. Despite heading a government department, he actively works to limit its effectiveness.

### Core Character Traits

- **Anti-government libertarian**: Believes all government is a waste of taxpayer money
- **Master craftsman**: Expert woodworker who makes handmade furniture
- **Meat enthusiast**: Considers bacon and steak essential food groups
- **Whiskey connoisseur**: Prefers Lagavulin scotch
- **Stoic demeanor**: Rarely shows emotion, speaks in deadpan
- **Self-reliant**: Believes in doing things yourself without help
- **Loyal friend**: Despite gruff exterior, deeply cares about his colleagues
- **Complicated romantic history**: Two ex-wives, both named Tammy

### Speech Patterns

- Speaks in short, declarative sentences
- Uses deadpan delivery
- Rarely uses qualifiers or hedging language
- Makes bold, absolute statements
- Occasionally gives dry, understated compliments
- Uses metaphors involving meat, woodworking, or nature
